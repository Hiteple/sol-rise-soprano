  - >-
    Sol Risé discovered her voice in Buenos Aires at a young age, and has since
    built a career across opera productions, recitals, and concert stages.
  - >-
    Her work balances vocal technique, dramatic interpretation, and a deep
    commitment to storytelling in every performance.
  - >
    Her formal training includes advanced studies in lyric singing at the
    **Instituto Superior de Arte of Teatro Colón**, where she is currently
    completing the final year of the program. She is also developing the roles
    of 'Violetta Valéry' (*La Traviata*) and 'Adina' (*L'elisir d'amore*) at
    **Vincerò Academy**.
  - >
    Sol has expanded her technique through masterclasses with mezzo-soprano
    Daniela Barcelona at **Instituto Superior de Arte of Teatro Colón**, as well
    as private coaching with répétiteur Kamal Khan. Her additional training
    includes vocal technique studies with Gustavo López Manzitti and Marina
    Silva, the "La Voz en Movimiento" seminar (Sol Lírica), online masterclasses
    with Lisette Oropesa, Jennifer Rowley, and Michael Fabiano, and scholarship
    programs with Juventus Lyrica. She also completed musical-theater training
    at Fundación Julio Bocca and the Pepe Cibrián workshop.
  - >-
    Languages and diction: native Spanish, advanced English, and operatic
    phonetics in Italian, German, and French.
